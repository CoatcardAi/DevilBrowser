Virtual Audio Device Setup Guide

For virtual microphone routing to work, you need a virtual audio bridge driver installed on your machine:

  1. Download and install VB-CABLE Virtual Audio Cable (Free, Windows/Mac).
  2. Restart your computer after installation.
  3. In this dropdown above, select CABLE Input (VB-Audio Virtual Cable) as the virtual output.
  4. In your targeted third-party application (e.g. Discord, Zoom, OBS), choose CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable) as your Microphone / Input Device.
  5. Click Start Routing to pipe browser/system audio directly into their microphone stream.
